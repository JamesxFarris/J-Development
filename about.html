Our Story

The Faces of J2

What started as a shared passion for building things on the web has grown into a full-fledged partnership dedicated to helping businesses thrive online.

We met through our mutual love of clean code and creative problem-solving. After collaborating on several projects, we realized our skills complemented each other perfectly. One brings the technical expertise, the other brings the design eye - together, we deliver complete digital solutions.

Based in Myrtle Beach, SC, we've made it our mission to provide boutique-level attention and big-agency results to every client we work with. We believe great websites aren't just built - they're crafted with intention, care, and a deep understanding of what makes your business unique.

James Farris

Founder & Developer

Originally from Luzerne, Pennsylvania, I made Myrtle Beach home during middle school and haven't looked back. I'm currently pursuing a degree in computer science and love channeling that passion into building real-world projects that solve real problems. When I'm not coding, you'll find me making music, staying active at the gym, or competing in online games. My background in customer service—from managing retail teams to working the front desk at a hotel—has taught me how to truly listen to what people need and communicate solutions clearly. I believe the best websites are built by people who genuinely enjoy helping others succeed.
